Our Office

Welcome to Dr. Harrington's office! We strive to provide your child with the highest quality dental care both thoroughly and efficiently in a relaxed and caring environment. Dr. Harrington is one of the few dual trained orthodontic and pediatric dentists on the central coast allowing us to monitor your child's teeth from general cleanings to braces. Our mission is to equip your child with the tools to maintain healthy teeth throughout their lives through education and prevention.
