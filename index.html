Douglas Harrington DDS

Dr. Harrington is a pediatric and orthodontic dentist who needed a new logo as he was moving to a new office. He wanted an aeronautical feel so I incorporated wings. I used the smile shape as the crossbar on the H to show they are a dentist.

Flowers by Kim

Flowers by Kim is an exclusive wedding and event florist in Paso Robles, California. I designed the logo and website with a minimalist style. This allows you to easily add the logo to anything or put anything on the website to bring out the natural beauty in flowers.

Lahargou Farms

Lahargou Farms sells eggs and chickens so they wanted a simple logo that could place on any of their packaging, labels, and apparel. I also created a potential website design when they were prepared to have an online presence.

Haymaker Inn

The Haymaker Inn is a Paso Robles, California vacation rental in the heart of vineyards. I created a script logo to reflect the high end feel yet in the middle of the country. Along with the logo I created a 10 page travel guide which includes information about the house, places to eat, things to do, and winery tour information.
