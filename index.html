Douglas Harrington DDS

Dr. Harrington is a pediatric and orthodontic dentist who needed a new logo as he was moving to a new office. He wanted an aeronautical feel so I incorporated wings. I used the smile shape as the crossbar on the H to show they are a dentist.

Flowers by Kim

Flowers by Kim is an exclusive wedding and event florist in Paso Robles, California. I designed the logo and website with a minimalist style. Using a minimalist style always you to easily let the colorful flowers make the impact and make the website and logo versatile, timeless feel.

Lahargou Farms

Lahargou Farms mainly sells eggs so the client wanted a simple logo that he could place on any of his packaging, labels, and apparel. I used a bold sans-serif typeface along with a simple icon so it could be easily read, even on an egg.

The Lab

The Lab is a free after school English and math tutoring program led by teachers at Paso Robles High School. There goal was to let students know about the program and how they could get help while keeping a professional yet 'cool' look to the brand. I created business cards, letterheads, internal forms and various posters.
